# Information Security Laboratory

Welcome to my Information Security Laboratory repository.

In this repository, I will document and implement all the tasks required for this lab, which are primarily based on the educational projects provided by the **SEED Project**.

---

### Lab Objective
The goal of this laboratory is to enhance the practical understanding of information security concepts and to apply the theories learned by simulating real-world digital attack and defense scenarios.

### Methodology
For each task, I will provide the following:
1.  **Task Description:** A clear explanation of the task's objective and what we are aiming to achieve or understand.
2.  **Implementation Steps:** A detailed presentation of the practical steps followed to complete the task.
3.  **Results and Analysis:** An explanation of what happened after executing the steps, along with an analysis of the resulting security implications.
